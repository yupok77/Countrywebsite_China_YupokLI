About Chinese Culture

Chinese culture is one of the oldest in the world, known for its rich traditions, philosophy, arts, and festivals. The culture emphasizes values such as respect, family, and community.

Chinese art, music, literature, and cinema have significantly influenced the world, and festivals like Chinese New Year and the Mid-Autumn Festival are celebrated by millions globally.
